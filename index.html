About

Tim Berners Lee is a british computer scientist who's best known for creating the World Wide Web (WWW), which revolutionized the way we communicate, share and retrieve information online today. Berners-Lee also created Hypertext Markup Language (HTML), Hypertext Transfer Protocol (HTTP) and the Uniform Resource Locator (URL) to support the W3.
