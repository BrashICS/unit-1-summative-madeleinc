History

The World Wide Web and Berners-Lee's other inventions almost immediately revolutionized the web after release, with thousands of webpages being created and millions of users browsing the web. To this day, all websites use and rely on these inventions to navigate web pages online.

In recognition of his importance, Tim Berners Lee was knighted in 2004 by Queen Elizabeth II for his contributions in developing the web.
